# StudyGroupFinder - Presentation Script (12 Pages)

Use this file as a simple, audience-friendly script for your presentation.

---

## Page 1 - Title

Project: StudyGroupFinder  
Tagline: Find your people, study better, stay consistent.

Short intro:  
StudyGroupFinder is a platform that helps students find the right study partners and turn individual effort into team momentum.

---

## Page 2 - The Real Problem

Many students want to study seriously, but face the same issues:

- They do not know who is the right person to study with
- Group coordination takes too much time
- Motivation drops without accountability
- Notes, planning, and discussion are scattered across many apps

Big point: The problem is not effort. The problem is structure.

---

## Page 3 - Vision

Our vision is to make studying more social, more focused, and more sustainable.

Instead of students asking, "Who can I study with today?", we want them to already have:

- A trusted study circle
- A clear routine
- A place to collaborate
- A way to track progress over time

---

## Page 4 - What StudyGroupFinder Does

StudyGroupFinder supports a complete study journey:

1. Students create a profile
2. They discover compatible partners
3. They form or join study groups
4. They communicate and plan together
5. They track study progress and stay accountable

This turns random studying into a consistent system.

---

## Page 5 - Key User Benefits

Why this is useful for students:

- Better partner quality, not just more contacts
- Faster group formation
- Clearer communication
- More consistent habits
- Stronger motivation from shared goals

Core value: Better outcomes with less coordination stress.

---

## Page 6 - Main Features (Non-Technical)

The current version includes:

- Account and personal profile setup
- Smart partner recommendations
- Study group creation and joining
- Live discussions for community and group collaboration
- Study progress tracking
- Personal notes to keep learning organized

---

## Page 7 - Demo Story

Presentation story to follow:

1. A student signs up and sets preferences
2. The platform suggests suitable partners
3. The student joins a study group
4. Group members chat and coordinate sessions
5. The student logs study activity
6. The student sees progress and stays motivated

This gives your audience a clear, human-centered flow.

---

## Page 8 - Why This Matters

This project is not only about features. It addresses a real behavior gap:

- Students know what to study
- But they struggle to stay consistent over weeks

StudyGroupFinder helps convert intention into routine through community and accountability.

---

## Page 9 - Challenges We Faced

Main product challenges:

- Making recommendations feel relevant
- Balancing social interaction with productivity
- Keeping the experience simple for first-time users
- Building trust so students return regularly

What we learned: Simplicity and clarity matter as much as functionality.

- Help students build long-term learning discipline

Long-term impact: Better academic outcomes through better habits.
